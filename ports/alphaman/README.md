## Controls

| Button | Action |
|--|--| 
|DPad/L Analog|Movement|
|B|Jump|
|A-Hold to Charge Secondary Weapon|Shoot1|
|Y|Dash|
|X|Secondary Weapon|
|L1/R1|Switch Secondary Weapon|
|Up|Climbing|
